CodeBerry was our dream to make computer science education approachable, exciting, and available to everyone.

Between 2016 and 2024, we helped 200,000 students from 43 countries, speaking 30 languages, take their first steps in the transformative world of programming. We are especially proud of the 15,000 who took a deeper dive, exploring the superpowers of coding with our guidance.

Unfortunately, not all worthwhile endeavours guarantee financial success as well. Despite our best efforts, we couldn't turn CodeBerry into a sustainable business, it was time to say goodbye. Our servers may have gone dark, we hope our passion for coding created sparks in the hearts of our alumni that will continue to light the way for many.

CodeBerry was made possible by the dedication of our 350 team members, the curiosity and trust of our students, and the support from our investors, mentors, friends, and families. A heartfelt thank you goes to each one of you for making this adventure memorable.
